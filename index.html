Welcome to OSR – A Landmark of Hospitality in Prayagraj

Located conveniently on M.G. Road near the High Court, Om Sai Ki Rasoi (OSR) is Prayagraj’s premier destination for exquisite vegetarian dining, comfortable luxury lodging, and unforgettable events. Whether you are hosting a grand wedding, seeking a peaceful weekend retreat, or craving authentic multi-cuisine flavors, OSR delivers world-class service with traditional Indian warmth.

Our Cuisine

North Indian Special

Savor the rich, authentic spices of North India. From creamy Dal Makhani to Lajawaab Koftas, our signature dishes are crafted to perfection.

South Indian Thali

Crispy dosas, fluffy idlis, and authentic chutneys. Experience the true, comforting taste of South India right here in UP.

Chinese Cuisine

A perfect blend of fiery woks and aromatic spices. Dive into our incredibly popular Indo-Chinese starters and sizzling platters.

Luxury Hotel Rooms

After a busy day exploring the city or attending meetings, retreat to our premium hotel rooms. Featuring modern amenities, plush bedding, 24/7 room service, and impeccable hygiene, OSR offers a tranquil oasis in the bustling Civil Lines district.

Grand Banquet Hall

Make your weddings, receptions, ring ceremonies, and corporate events truly spectacular. Our fully air-conditioned banquet hall offers elegant décor, customizable catering menus, and ample parking space to ensure your celebration is flawless
